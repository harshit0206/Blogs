SHIPPING LABEL

A shipping label describes and identifies the contents of a container or shipment, as well as its origin, destination, weight, and carrier's name. The tracking number, date, package quantity, weight, address, and Ship Street, city and state are all important pieces of information. Each label is unique, and old labels cannot be used. A fresh label must be created for each delivery.

The firm can monitor and control its orders with shipping labels, providing a positive customer experience, increasing brand loyalty and customer retention, and eventually increasing revenues. Packages might be delayed in transit, sent to the wrong location, or lost entirely without a proper mailing label. Any of these circumstances might result in a financial loss or a loss of consumer confidence, thus the shipping label is important.

Information on shipping label

This is the information you may get from the shipping label if you pay close attention.


Buyer details
  1. Name
  2. Address
  3. Phone number
Carrier details
  1. Name of courier company
  2. AWB number
  3. Routing code
Product details
  1. Dimensions of product
  2. Payment method
  3. Weight of product
  4. Item details
Seller details
  1. Name
  2. Address
  3. Mobile number
  4. Order ID

Orders without a shipping label may be delayed in transit, given to the incorrect address, or lost entirely. Any of these instances leads to an economic loss or a loss of consumer confidence, highlighting the necessity of a proper shipping label.


Importance of shipping labels
  • Efficient transportation

    Due to the existence of a shipping label, the item may be sorted using an automatic sorting system. As a result of the sorting mechanism, the shipment can be cleared quickly. The shipping label ensures that the package reaches the intended recipient in a timely manner. It also lowers costs for businesses, increases productivity, and boosts customer happiness.

  • Accurate as compared to the manual input

    A barcode can be seen on shipping labels. The barcode is a very efficient and cost-effective method of data storage. It's less time-consuming than the manual entry technique, which is prone to human mistake.

  • Helps customers in tracking

    Customers are constantly looking for a simple and convenient way to track their shipments. Company may provide its clients with an easy method to monitor their goods by using a shipping label. The tracking information provided on the delivery label may simply be used to monitor the product. Firm may keep its clients updated on the status of their cargo at every stage of the delivery process this way.

  • Lesser delivery time

    The courier agency's workload is also reduced when all required information is provided on the shipping label. All they have to do is scan the label to get all the information about the consumer. When opposed to handwritten labels, printed labels have a lower risk of mistake. This ensures that the product is delivered on time and saves time.

  • Streamline the process

    Company may simplify its packaging and shipping procedure by printing shipping labels. It will save hours each week, as well as a lot of unnecessary pressure, if firm can rapidly print big quantities of professional-looking labels and simply attach them to shipments.
