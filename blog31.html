In this article, I'll explain you how to use the DRPU Barcode Label Maker Software for Mac to create a barcode. Although it is a technical solution, it does not need programming skills. I've gone over every stage of the barcode creation process. So, to print a barcode on your Mac, start by downloading the software.

Step 2: From the Batch Processing tab at the top, import your Excel file containing Barcode information into the software.


Step 3: You may alternatively use separate Excel files to import Barcode Value, Barcode Header, and Barcode Footer.

Step 4: To generate rapid barcodes and customized barcode labels, choose between Quick Barcode Mode and Barcode Designing View Mode respectively from the top.



Quick Barcode Mode

Step 5(i): Select the Barcode font you want and check Enable Batch Processing.


Step 5(ii): Select the value of the barcode, as well as the header and footer. You may also change the values in the Header and Footer sections.

Step 5(iii): For the Barcodes, you can adjust the General settings, Font, and Color options.

Barcode Designing View Mode

Step 5(a): From the very left side, add a new Barcode and navigate to its properties.


Step 5(b): Select a barcode font and choose Data Source as Batch Processing Series.


Step 5(c): Select the value of the barcode, as well as the header and footer. You may also change the values in the Header and Footer sections.

Step 5(d): For the barcodes, you can adjust the size, font, and color.

Step 5(e): Add text, shapes, images, and other elements to your barcode stickers.


Step 6: Use print settings to print your barcodes, and your barcodes are now ready to use.
