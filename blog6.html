Benefits of barcodes in supply chain management:

In distribution industry, the supply chain must produce accurate information, or productivity and profitability will suffer.

The high-priority thing within an industry is being able to know where an item is within the supply chain at any point of time. This could include a finished product or raw material.

1.  Tracking & Inventory Management
2.  Enhancing Accuracy
3.  Transparency in the Process


I. Tracking & Inventory Management

Barcode provides instant access to a large amount of data. It also connects workers and truck drivers to manage paperwork, tracking, and managing inventory efficiently.


II. Enhancing Accuracy

With barcode scanning, error corrections can be made in real-time. Even if a package label is destroyed, it is available to complete transactions.


III. Transparency in Process

Supply chain is managed more effectively using barcodes in real-time. Tracing products becomes more efficient using barcode scanning technology.


Consider these benefits of barcoding
  • Item location is known at all times, reduces product search time, improves inventory stocks, and enhances control of manufacturing process.

  • Improves work productivity and reduces finished goods cost.

  • Enables monitoring of production, order fulfillment, and distribution processes in real-time.

  • Reduces inventory costs and reduces labor costs by eliminating manual steps.

  • Increases shipping accuracy. Helps to ensure that orders ship completely, error-free, and on time, thus improves satisfaction of customers.


OPTIMIZE THE PROCESS WHILE GAINING PROFITS

Barcoding provides Return Of Investment by reducing processing and data entry time, thus improves the performance of business systems. As a result, industry can track items accurately and attain supply chain visibility. Manufacturing operations in industry can gain more process optimization. Distribution centers can accomplish more efficiency and control, from inventory, to picking, shipping, and customer services. The results are reduced costs, smooth production, and higher profit.
