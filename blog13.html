The production and supply chain in manufacturing industry depends on real-time data to provide information to the whole manufacturing operations. As more companies turned for improvement in processes and reduction in costs, barcoding helps in enhancing their manufacturing system.

The barcodes provide quick and accurate data, which is essential for the efficient and fast operation of the production line.


Automation in manufacturing with barcodes:

In manufacturing process, barcoding is used to identify parts or containers of ingredients, quality control, batch tracking and finished products for shipping.

Implementation of barcodes provides multiple benefits, including costs saving and less typing when entering the data. Instead of a warehouse worker conforming an item number and then might entering the wrong item number, s/he can just take a barcode scanner and use it for the correct item number.


Manufacturing Applications of barcodes:
  1. Receiving

    Raw materials arriving in a manufacturing industry have barcode labels on their packaging to meet the industryâ€™s requirements for materials management.

  2. Raw Material Inventory

    The worker scans both the package and the storage location to transfer the material to an inventory location and the data automatically recorded in the system.

  3. Picking

    The workers scan the barcodes to retrieve raw materials from inventory and complete the transfer operation.

  4. Work in process

    Labels on each item are scanned to confirm the right parts and quantities are picked. Additional barcode labels can be applied to identify the item by its new part number. Barcodes are used to identify between batches and lots.

  5. Finished Goods

    Barcodes are scanned to identify the goods. If no sales order for a product exists, it is taken to a warehouse location for inventory. On order, it is retrieved from the finished goods inventory with the help barcode labels.

  6. Shipping

    In the shipping department, the barcode on the item is scanned and new labels are applied with shipping information. The labels ensure right delivery of product and to track progress during shipment.
