Barcodes in advertising industry


The modern technology revolution has resulted in increasing competitiveness in terms of innovation and the development of new advertising tactics. As a result of this, barcodes began to appear in companies. Because of its quick reading, the barcode system is becoming increasingly popular in marketing and new advertising trends for products and services.

Advertisers are utilizing barcodes to communicate with customers in a more engaging, fascinating, and distinctive manner. Simply by downloading and installing an app that can scan barcodes on a Smartphone, customer may learn a lot more about the goods being promoted. 2D barcodes are increasingly being used in advertising and promotion by businesses.

QR Codes in advertisements

The QR code connects the physical and online worlds. Advertisers can use QR codes on advertisements, posters, and other printed materials to send people to mobile landing sites with far more information and interactivity than is possible on a printed page.



Working of QR Codes

A QR Code is read by scanning it using a mobile device equipped with a camera and a QR Code reader application. When a QR Code is scanned using the device's camera, it is converted into usable data, such as a text message or a mobile web page.


Once a QR code is scanned the encoded information can contain:

  • Web page URL
  • Contact information
  • A message
  • Email address
  • Google Maps Location
  • Social Media profile link
